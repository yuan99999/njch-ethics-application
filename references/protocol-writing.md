# Protocol Writing

Use this when drafting or revising a hospital research protocol.

## Recommended Structure

1. Title page: study title, project source, PI, contact, phone, study period, version/date.
2. Rationale: clinical background, knowledge gap, why the study can be answered with existing clinical data.
3. Objectives: primary objective and secondary/exploratory objectives.
4. Study design: single/multicenter, retrospective/prospective, observational/interventional, randomization/blinding if any.
5. Study population: age range, disease/procedure setting, sites, sample size basis.
6. Inclusion/exclusion criteria.
7. Data sources and variables: routine records, monitoring records, labs, medications, outcomes, timing anchors.
8. Statistical plan: descriptive analysis, association models, adjustment variables, sensitivity analyses, missing-data handling.
9. Risk/benefit: routine care versus research-only procedures, minimal-risk justification, privacy risks.
10. Ethics and consent: consent or waiver plan, pediatric protections, confidentiality.
11. Data management: de-identification, access permissions, storage, retention/archive.
12. Timeline, deliverables, research team table, PI commitment/signature area.

## Writing Principles

- Say “本研究为回顾性病历资料研究，不改变诊疗流程，不增加检查、治疗、采血或随访” for data-only retrospective studies when true.
- State that identifiers are used only for internal verification and are removed before analysis.
- For exploratory observational analyses, avoid causal language. Prefer “相关”“关联”“探索性分析”.
- For clinical thresholds, state they are used for research stratification and not as standalone diagnostic criteria.
- Avoid exposing operational data-cleaning details in the protocol unless they affect participant risk, privacy, or consent.

## Useful Chinese Wording

Retrospective design:
“本研究为单中心回顾性队列研究，拟在伦理委员会批准后，基于医院既往临床诊疗过程中形成的病历及常规监测资料开展分析。”

No extra burden:
“研究不改变受试者既往或当前诊疗流程，不增加额外检查、治疗、采血、随访或经济负担。”

Sample size:
“拟调取符合初筛条件的既往病例资料约X例，最终分析队列依据预设纳入排除标准、关键变量完整性及数据可分析性确定。”

Publication:
“研究结果将以汇总形式发表或报告，不公开姓名、住院号、病历号等可识别身份信息。”
