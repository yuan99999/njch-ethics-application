# Submission Checklist

Use this when the user asks what to prepare or whether the ethics package is complete.

## Must Prepare

- Online initial ethics application form in the hospital system.
- Research protocol using the hospital template, with version number and version date.
- Waiver of informed consent application, if requesting waiver.
- Informed consent form when direct consent is planned or required; use guardian consent and child assent language when minors are contacted and age/capacity make assent appropriate.
- PI and research team information: name, department/unit, title, phone/email, role, ethics/GCP training status if requested.
- Study title, study period, study type, study site, sample size, target population, inclusion/exclusion criteria.
- Privacy and data security plan: identifiers used only for internal verification, de-identification before analysis, access control, storage, retention/archive plan.

## Project-Dependent

- Informed consent form and guardian consent form.
- Child assent language, if participants are contacted and age/capacity make assent appropriate.
- Recruitment materials, questionnaire, interview outline, follow-up script, CRF/data extraction table.
- Biospecimen plan: source, volume, storage, export/sharing, secondary use, disposal.
- Drug/device/IVD materials: instructions, registration/investigational documents, sponsor/CRO documents, insurance/compensation.
- For drug/device/IVD trial initial review: use the matching initial review worksheet/application form from `assets/official-forms/drug-device-trials/`, plus protocol, synopsis, ICF, recruitment material, CRF, investigator brochure or device/IVD information, safety monitoring plan, sponsor/CRO qualifications, investigator CV/GCP certificates, insurance/compensation documents, and contracts/agreements if required locally.
- For follow-up events: use the matching amendment, annual follow-up, noncompliance/protocol deviation, SAE, suspension/early termination, or close-out form from `assets/official-forms/drug-device-trials/`.
- For new technology/new project scenarios: check `assets/official-forms/medical-ethics/新技术新项目医学伦理申请-知情同意书.doc`.
- Cooperation agreement, funding statement, conflict-of-interest statement, third-party qualifications.

## Confirm With Ethics Office

- Whether PI CV, GCP certificate, or department approval upload is mandatory for this submission type.
- Whether the system requires a signed scanned waiver form in addition to the electronic form.
- Whether retrospective data-only studies require a separate data extraction sheet.
- Whether hospital-specific templates have been updated since the user-provided files.
- Whether the public website templates, 红帆下载中心 templates, and ethics office requirements are fully aligned for the current submission cycle.

## Final Checks

- File names, version numbers, and dates are consistent.
- Signature/stamp/committee opinion fields remain blank unless signed/stamped originals are provided.
- The protocol does not mention internal data-cleaning disputes, chart-entry inconsistencies, or non-submission technical notes unless ethically necessary.
- Use `references/official-sources.md` and `assets/official-forms/manifest.json` to verify local template source and fetch date when citing or reusing bundled templates.
